# IV.F: Professional development opportunities

## Domain: Professional Role: Behavior, Capabilities, and Leadership (15%)

### [Status]: 🟢 Studied

### [Technician Perspective]
**Professional Development Opportunities:** Stay current through conferences (IMSH, SimOps, SimGHOSTS), webinars, and certifications (CHSOS, CHSE). Network with the global simulation community to share best practices and solve technical challenges.

### [Clinical Application]
Continuous professional development ensures you are using the most efficient and evidence-based techniques to support clinical training.

### [Key Terminology]
*   **IMSH:** International Meeting on Simulation in Healthcare.
*   **SSH (Society for Simulation in Healthcare):** The primary professional body for healthcare simulation.
*   **SimGHOSTS:** A group focused specifically on simulation technology specialists.

### [Exam Scenario]
**Q:** Your center is implementing a new AR/VR system, but you have no experience with it. What is your first step?
**A:** Identify professional development resources such as manufacturer webinars, SSH special interest groups (SIGs), or peer networking to build competency.
