# II.B.2: Healthcare equipment recommendations

## Domain: Simulation Technology Operations (35%)

### [Status]: 🟢 Studied

### [Technician Perspective]
**Recommend healthcare equipment for use:**
*   Balance realism with budget. A $30k real ventilator might not be necessary if a $5k simulated ventilator meets the learning objectives.
*   Ensure compatibility: Will the real IV pump connect properly to the manikin's fluid system?

### [Clinical Application]
Using the exact same model of IV pump in the sim lab as the hospital uses on the floor reduces cognitive load and improves transfer of skills.

### [Key Terminology]
*   **Transfer of Training:** Applying skills learned in sim to the real world.

### [Exam Scenario]
**Q:** A faculty member wants to purchase a new brand of defibrillator for the sim lab. What is your primary recommendation?
**A:** Purchase the exact same make and model used in the clinical environment the learners will be working in.

### [Notes & Lab Application]
*   Audit your clinical equipment against the hospital's current standard.
