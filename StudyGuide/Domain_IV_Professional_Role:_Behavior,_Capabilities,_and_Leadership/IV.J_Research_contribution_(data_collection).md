# IV.J: Research contribution (data collection)

## Domain: Professional Role: Behavior, Capabilities, and Leadership (15%)

### [Status]: 🟢 Studied

### [Technician Perspective]
**Research Contribution:** Support data collection by exporting software logs, managing video data, and ensuring 'Technical Consistency' for every research subject. If the simulator behaves differently for Group A than Group B, the research is invalid.

### [Clinical Application]
Rigorous simulation research leads to improved clinical protocols and better patient outcomes.

### [Key Terminology]
*   **Software Logs:** Digital records of every action taken in the simulator software.
*   **Standardization:** Ensuring every research participant experiences the exact same technical environment.

### [Exam Scenario]
**Q:** You are assisting with a research study on nurse response times. During one session, the Wi-Fi drops and the manikin lags. What is the impact?
**A:** The data for that session is compromised due to a lack of 'Technical Consistency' and may need to be excluded from the study.
