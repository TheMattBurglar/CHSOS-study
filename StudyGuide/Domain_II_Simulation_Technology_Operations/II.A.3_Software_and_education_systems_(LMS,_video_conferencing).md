# II.A.3: Software and education systems (LMS, video conferencing)

## Domain: Simulation Technology Operations (35%)

### [Status]: 🟢 Studied

### [Technician Perspective]
**Software and Education Systems:**
*   **LMS (Learning Management System):** Used for pre-brief materials, scheduling, and tracking completion.
*   **Video Conferencing:** Zoom/Teams integration requires capturing the AV system's output as a virtual webcam.
*   **Simulator Software:** LLEAP, Maestro, Muse. Often requires specific OS versions and dedicated graphics.

### [Clinical Application]
LMS integration ensures learners have completed prerequisites (like medication math) before arriving at a high-stakes simulation.

### [Key Terminology]
*   **SCORM / xAPI:** Standards for elearning software communication.
*   **SSO (Single Sign-On):** Security and convenience for users.

### [Exam Scenario]
**Q:** You need to stream a simulation to a remote classroom. Which setup ensures the lowest latency?
**A:** A hardwired capture card directly encoding the camera feeds to the streaming PC, bypassing wireless networks.
